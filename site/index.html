📖 Librarian

Drop in your documents. Get back a clean, classified, searchable library. Everything stays on your Mac.

Download for Apple Silicon Download for Intel Macs

macOS 14 or newer · free & open source · all releases

🗂 Drag & drop anything

PDFs, Word documents, Markdown, plain text, transcripts, and scanned images — drop them anywhere in the window.

✨ Cleaned & classified

Documents are converted, cleaned, and filed under Dewey-style labels automatically, with live progress for every step.

🔍 Instant full-text search

Search the cleaned library with highlighted snippets, then export results as Markdown, text, or JSON.

🔒 Local first

The whole engine runs inside the app on your Mac. Nothing leaves your machine unless you connect an LLM provider.

Install in three steps

  1. Download the DMG for your Mac (Apple menu → About This Mac shows your chip).
  2. Open it and drag Librarian into Applications.
  3. Launch Librarian and drop in your first file.

First launch on an unsigned build: right-click the app, choose Open, then Open again — or allow it under System Settings → Privacy & Security.
